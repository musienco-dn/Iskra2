INSTRUCTIONS:

- Add an Emerald AI Saver to any AIs whose state you want to save. 
  This will save the AI's position, health, and behavior.

- Add an Emerald AI Health Monitor to any AIs whose health you want
  to monitor. You can assign event handlers and Message System messages
  when the AI is damaged and/or killed.
